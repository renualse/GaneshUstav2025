Why Ganesh Utsav is Celebrated?

Ganesh Chaturthi celebrates Lord Ganesha, the remover of obstacles.

He is also the god of wisdom and prosperity.

The festival was popularized by Lokmanya Bal Gangadhar Tilak.

Purpose: To unite people and promote cultural pride.

Devotees install idols, offer prayers, and enjoy decorations and festivities.
